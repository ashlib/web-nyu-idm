Forced into hiding, Aelin was raised as an assassin, where she took on the alias Celaena Sardothien and grew her infmaous reputation until captured and enslaved by the King of Adarlan. She was able to escape via a royal competition and eventually reclaimed her name and throne. click to close

  • As part Fae, Aelin has many abilities to her disposal, the most prevalent one being fire magic. As a descendant of Mala the Fire-Bringer, Aelin's fire magic is extremely powerful and near unlimited. Additionally, she has a touch of water magic from her mother's side, although it has barely manifested.
