• Yrene is a mixture of shades of gold and brown. Her skin and eyes are both described as golden brown and her curly hair is a rich brown with flecks of dark gold. Many refer to her as the Golden Lady. click to close

  • Yrene is level-headed, intelligent, and good-natured. Despite the cruelties she's endured, she believes there are better methods and solutions than violence. click to close

  • Yrene comes from a long-line of healers in her family. At the age of eleven, during the King of Adarlan's campaign to conquer the continent, Yrene's mother was murdered by soldiers. Escaping, Yrene fled to live with relatives before departing in order to become a healer at the Torre Cesme, an academy and home of the world's most gifted healers. click to close

  • Discovering that her healing abilities have the power to vanquish the Valg (dark creatures from another world), Yrene sets out with the Antican army (a vast and mighty kingdom in the Southern continent) to join the war against the Valg king Erawan. Within her may be the key to vanquishing Erawan once and for all. click to close

  • As a healer, Yrene possesses magic that can cure and/or treat most ailments, including diseases, illnesses, and physical injuries. Yrene's healing magic can also vanquish Valg creatures who have possessed human or fae bodies. She also knows basic self-defense and has spent her leisure time training other young women. click to close
